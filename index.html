What are you studying in college?

I am studying chemical engineering. This field interested me because I had a great chemistry teacher in high school so I liked chemistry, and I also liked math. The combination of chemistry and math as a chemical engineer seemed like a good fit so I chose that as my major. The amount of different fields chemical engineers work in also appealed to me because I didn’t know exactly what I wanted to do after graduation.

Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I have always been interested in computers and enjoyed my introduction to programming class in college. Web development appealed to me because it is a very useful skill to have. I plan on traveling after graduation and would love to have a skill that enables me to work online.

What activities do you do outside of school?

I am a VP of AIESEC in Boston. We are an international non-profit working to provide leadership experience to students. We provide this experience by sending and receiving students to and from other countries to work or volunteer. I work in the outgoing volunteer program so I send people from the United States abroad to help those less fortunate.
