Past Internships

I did a couple six month internships while at Northesatern. The first one was at E-Ink doing research on electronic paper display screens. While there I worked under a senior physicist helping collect and analyze lab data. My second internship was at the Town of Wellesley Engineering Department. This opportunity gave me exposure to some civil engineering design and practices. While working there I used AutoCAD and ArcGIS to update and create maps.

Camp Mondamin

Growing up I went to away to Camp Mondamin every summer for six weeks. Camp Mondamin is an outdoors camp for boys 8 to 17 focused on taking trips in kayaking, mountain biking, climbing, and hiking. The skills needed to live away from my parents and a love of mountain biking are two things that are largely due to going to this camp. Mondamin influenced me to go to highschool at Colorado Rocky Mountain School which is a boarding school focused on outdoor education located in Carbondale, Colorado. After my Freshman and Junior years at Northeastern I worked for three months at this camp teaching mountain biking. The photos in the carousel are from Summer 2016.
